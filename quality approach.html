The System Book

The System Book is a handbook developed to define the strategic features of the UNYT , to organize operational processes, to ensure continuous improvement, and to create a source of information about operations. Based on the Plan → Activate → Check → Improve (PACI) methodology, the System Book reflects a process-oriented approach. Possible complexity is avoided by referencing the written rules and current regulations governing the modern university system in the System Book.

The System Book is published online and is available digitally without the need to convert it to print. A clear and simple language is used in the System Book.

System Book v2.3.1, which was prepared with the joint contribution of Administration, is updated openly to the feedback of internal and external stakeholders of the University. The version number of the System Book is changed depending on the levels of updates: Updates take effect at the discretion of University management. If the feedback made by the stakeholders of the University creates a need for updating, it can be included in the System Book after it is approved by the administration.
