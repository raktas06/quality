ABOUT UNYT

UNYT is the first private university in Albania, founded in 2002, and the only institution in Albania that offers double degrees (American and Albanian) for Bachelor Programs in cooperation with the State University of New York/Empire State College – USA. Since 2002, UNYT has offered 4-year study programs. In 2020, by the decision of the Senate and the Board, UNYT directed the official request to the Ministry of Education for institutional reorganization from a four-year program to a three-year Bologna system. By Order no. 2350/2 of the Ministry of Education, UNYT received approval to offer a three-year program (Bologna System). From 2020 onwards, UNYT offers three years of study (Bachelor), five years (Integrated Master of Science 3+2), and two years (Master of Science).
