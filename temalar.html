Themes

The System theme is a focal point that forms the basis for all the other components of the System Book. Surrounding the system theme are other themes (Research, Education and Society) to cover it. The core is a dynamic mechanism that works from the inside out, where core competencies and key strategies are defined.The system is considered as the focus where the core competencies of the UNYT are defined, its main strategy is specified, its competitive structure is built, its difference from other units of the university is revealed, and the future of the structure is discussed.

The first of the priority areas of the UNYT, the Research theme deals with the creation, development and dissemination of scientific knowledge, which is the main activity for a scientific institution.

Global university education is defined by the theme Education as the second priority area of ​​the UNYT. It contains all the information about the training processes.

The creation of scientific value for society through scientific activities is the subject of the theme Society. With the development of entrepreneurship and university-industry relations, it supports the emergence of a social structure aimed at science.
